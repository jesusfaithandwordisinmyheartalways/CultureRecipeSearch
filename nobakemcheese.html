No Bake American Mac & Cheese

No Baked Macaroni and Cheese.

No-bake macaroni and cheese is faster to prepare than the baked version, making it an ideal option for busy individuals or weeknight dinners when time is limited.

The no-bake method often results in an ultra-creamy, smooth cheese sauce that coats the pasta thoroughly, providing a rich and indulgent mouthfeel.

The recipe is straightforward and requires fewer steps and ingredients, making it accessible to novice cooks and those looking for a simple, satisfying meal without the complexity of baking.

All cooking is done on the stovetop, eliminating the need for an oven. This is particularly beneficial during hot weather or in homes without oven access.

Without the baking step, there's less risk of overcooking or drying out the dish. The stovetop method ensures the pasta remains perfectly cooked and the sauce maintains its desired consistency.

Step 1:

Heat a large saucepot with a drizzle of olive oil and add the onion, garlic, bell pepper and cilantro and let saut until the onion is clear

Step 2:

Add the frozen creamed spinach and continue to saut until the spinach is thawed

Step 3:

Add the ketchup and stir, continuing to saut on low.In a food processor combine the cottage cheese, milk and 4 oz. of the shredded cheddar cheese. Process until the cottage is smooth.Prepare the macaroni as directed.

Step 4:

Add the cooked macaroni to the saucepot with the sauting spinach and toss add the processed cottage cheese mixture and toss. Turn off heat. Top with the remaining 4 oz. of cheddar cheese. Cover and let the cheddar cheese melt. This takes only a second or two.

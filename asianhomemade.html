Asian Homemade Creamy Ramen Soup Recipe

Asian Homemade Creamy Ramen Soup

The creamy broth, often made with ingredients like miso paste, soy sauce, or coconut milk, provides a rich and savory base that is deeply satisfying. The umami flavor profile of the soup is enhanced by toppings like tender meats, soft-boiled eggs, and fresh vegetables.

The creamy consistency of the broth offers a comforting and indulgent mouthfeel that is both soothing and satisfying, making it a perfect comfort food, especially during colder months.

Homemade ramen often includes a variety of fresh, nutritious ingredients such as vegetables (bok choy, mushrooms, carrots), proteins (chicken, tofu, eggs), and aromatics (garlic, ginger). These ingredients provide essential vitamins, minerals, and protein, contributing to a balanced and wholesome meal.

One of the appealing aspects of homemade ramen is the ability to customize it according to personal preferences. People can adjust the ingredients, spiciness, and toppings to create their perfect bowl of soup, catering to various dietary needs and taste preferences.

The hot, creamy broth of ramen soup is incredibly warming and comforting, making it a popular choice for those seeking a hearty and soothing meal. It's often associated with feelings of coziness and relaxation.

Instructions

Step 1:

In a food processor combine onions, carrots, and garlic and mince well. The pieces should be very small.

Step 2:

Heat oil in a soup pot

Step 3:

Add minced vegetables and saute, mixing occasionally, until just soft [7 - 10 minutes].

Step 4:

Add flour, poultry seasoning, turmeric, sea salt, and celery seed and stir constantly for about 30 seconds, coating the vegetables well

Step 5:

Add kombu if you are using it. Bring to boil and then simmer, covered, for 30 minutes.After 30 minutes, pour in non-dairy milk and noodles

Step 6:

Let simmer an additional 10 minutes or until noodles are soft."

Step 7:

Remove from heat and serve immediately."

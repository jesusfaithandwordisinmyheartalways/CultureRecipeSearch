Benefits of Pasta Shells With Ricotta Cheese Stuffing?

Nutritional Value

  • High in Protein:Ricotta cheese provides a good source of protein, essential for muscle repair, growth, and overall body function.
  • Calcium-Rich:Ricotta and other cheeses used in the dish are rich in calcium, important for maintaining strong bones and teeth
  • Vitamins and Minerals:Ricotta cheese contains essential vitamins and minerals such as vitamin A, vitamin B12, and zinc.

Balanced Meal

  • Combining carbohydrates from the pasta, protein and fats from the cheese, and vitamins and minerals from added vegetables or sauces creates a well-rounded and balanced meal.
  • If whole grain pasta is used or if the dish includes vegetables like spinach, it adds dietary fiber, aiding in digestion and promoting satiety.

Comfort Food Appeal

  • Baked pasta dishes provide warmth and comfort, making them ideal for colder weather or when seeking a comforting meal
  • The creamy, rich filling and tender pasta create a satisfying and indulgent dining experience.

Versatility and Customizability

  • The ricotta filling can be enhanced with various ingredients like spinach, garlic, ground meat, or different herbs and spices to suit personal tastes and dietary needs.
  • Can be paired with a variety of sauces, such as marinara, Alfredo, or a simple butter and herb sauce, allowing for diverse flavor profiles.

Health Benefits

  • The fiber, potassium, and low-fat content of the dish support cardiovascular health.
  • High in fiber and protein, which promote a feeling of fullness and help in weight management.

Ease of Preparation

  • The dish is relatively easy to prepare, making it accessible to home cooks of all skill levels.
  • The stuffed shells can be assembled in advance and baked when needed, making it convenient for meal planning, busy schedules, and entertaining.

Visual and Aromatic Appeal

  • The stuffed pasta shells have an attractive and elegant appearance, suitable for both family dinners and special occasions
  • The combination of baking pasta, cheese, and herbs creates an inviting aroma that enhances anticipation and appetite

Health Benefits

  • When made without meat, it offers a hearty and satisfying meal for vegetarians
  • Ricotta cheese contains healthy fats that contribute to a balanced diet and help in the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins

Step 1:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Beat Ricotta, Prosciutto and egg together in a large bowl. Stuff approximately 2 tablespoons of the cheese mixture into each shell and place in a baking pan. Cover with aluminum foil and bake 15 to 20 minutes. Arrange shells on serving plates and pour hot sauce over.

Ingredients
1 (16 oz.) box large shells, cooked" 1 pound Ricotta cheese 1/4 cup diced Prosciutto, cooked 1 egg, lightly beaten 2 cups hot tomato sauce
